Tic-Tac-Toe

This is a game called tic-tac-toe. The rules are very simple. firts you gotta tic, then you gotta tac, no hear comes the tricky part, then you gotta toe. You are assigned either an x or an o. Players take turns placing letters on the board. Get three of your letters lineed up in a row, before your opponents can to win.

Keep track of how many games you have played by signing up. You can see the number in the message box by clicking on the game index button.

Enjoy!
